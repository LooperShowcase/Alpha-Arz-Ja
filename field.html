Camp Nou (Catalan pronunciation: , meaning new field, often referred to in English as the Nou Camp), officially branded as Spotify Camp Nou for sponsorship reasons, is a football stadium in Barcelona, Spain. It has been the home stadium of FC Barcelona since its completion in 1957. With a current seating capacity of 99,354,[11] it is the largest stadium in Spain and Europe, and the third largest association football stadium in the world.
